# 🧠 Ideas Archive

**My creative vault** – where I preserve every wild idea that's ever struck me since high school.

## My Story

Back in high school, I fell in love with computers. Not just using them, but **what they could become**. An idea would spark – a blinking light on a Raspberry Pi, a voice assistant that actually understood me, a garden that watered itself – and I **couldn't let go**.

I'd dive in headfirst. Code late into the night. Tinker until it worked. No matter how random or impractical, if it grabbed me, **I built it**. This archive holds those raw experiments – exactly as I left them when the next idea pulled me away.

## What You'll Find

**Unfiltered snapshots of curiosity:**
- High school weekend projects
- 2AM "what if" prototypes  
- Ideas that wouldn't let me sleep
- Experiments I chased to completion

Each file is a time capsule – messy code, half-finished docs, the thrill of creation frozen in time.
